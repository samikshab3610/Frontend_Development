Smart Resume Scoring

Automatically evaluates your resume based on clarity, structure, keywords, and ATS-friendliness. Provides a clear score so you know exactly where you stand.

Identifies the most important keywords from the job description and shows which ones your resume is missing.

Highlights weak sections like missing skills, unclear summaries, or poor formatting, and gives actionable suggestions for improvement.

Compares your resume with the uploaded job description and tells you how well they align.
